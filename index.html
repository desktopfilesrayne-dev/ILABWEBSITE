🔬 Welcome to ILAB

The Interactive Laboratory (ILAB) is a virtual environment designed for students to explore, experiment, and learn in a digital space.

📘 About

ILAB provides simulations and guided activities that make learning more engaging, interactive, and accessible anytime. It serves as an essential virtual pre-orientation platform to bridge safety gaps and elevate laboratory competency among senior high school STEM students.
